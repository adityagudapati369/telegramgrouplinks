❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Group Links

What is Groupverse and what are group links? ＋

Groupverse is a free group links directory where you can discover and join active WhatsApp groups, Telegram groups, and Telegram channels. Group links are invite URLs that let anyone join a community on WhatsApp or Telegram instantly. Browse 1000+ group links by category, country, and language — no sign-up needed.

How do I join a WhatsApp group using a group link? ＋

Browse the WhatsApp group links on Groupverse, click the Join button on any group card, and you will be redirected to WhatsApp. Tap Join Group in the WhatsApp app to instantly become a member. No GroupVerse account is required.

Are all group links on Groupverse completely free? ＋

Yes. Every group link on Groupverse is 100% free. There is no registration, no subscription, and no hidden charges to browse or join any WhatsApp group link or Telegram group link through our directory.

How do I submit my group link to Groupverse? ＋

Click the Submit Group button at the top. Enter your group's name, invite link, category, country, language, and a short description. Your group link will be reviewed and listed in the directory after approval.

What categories of group links are available? ＋

Groupverse has group links across 17+ categories: Technology & AI, Cryptocurrency, Business & Startups, Education, Gaming, Health & Wellness, Jobs & Freelance, Sports, Travel, Entertainment, Music, News, Art & Design, and more — for both WhatsApp and Telegram.

Can I find Telegram group links and channel links here? ＋

Yes. Groupverse lists both WhatsApp group links and Telegram group links, including Telegram channel links. Use the Type filter to switch between WhatsApp Groups, WhatsApp Channels, Telegram Groups, and Telegram Channels.
